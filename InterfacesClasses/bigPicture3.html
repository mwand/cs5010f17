Functional vs OO Organization

Here's another way to visualize the same thing. The six blank rectangles correspond to our six pieces of functionality.

Functional: Circle Square Composite
weight
add-to-scene
OO: Circle Square Composite
weight
add-to-scene

In the functional organization, all of the pieces corresponding to weight are written together (symbolized here by the blue rectangles), and all of the pieces corresponding to add-to-scene are written together (symbolized here by the fuchsia rectangles).

In the object-oriented orientation, all the pieces for Circle are written together (the red rectangles), all the pieces for Square are written together (olive), and all the pieces for Composite are written together (turquoise).
